Static overlays
  • 2016–2023 frequency: How often each pixel was wet (share of months). High values = recurrent water.
  • Post–pre change: Difference in wetness between 2021–2023 and 2016–2018 (proportion of months). Blue = wetter recently; red = drier.
  • Main channel: Persistent river/water from JRC Global Surface Water (occurrence ≥60%).
  • Channel buffer: ~150 m around the main channel to focus off-channel activity.

How to use

  1. Open Layers (▦), choose a Dynamic layer. A short description appears below.
  2. Use the timeline to step through quarters or press ▶ to auto-play.
  3. Toggle Static overlays for context and adjust opacity.
  4. Turn on National boundary and Place labels from the left panel.
